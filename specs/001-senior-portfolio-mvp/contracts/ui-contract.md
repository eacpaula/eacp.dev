# Contract: Homepage Section Composition

## Purpose

This contract defines the expected public section order and minimum content responsibilities for the MVP homepage.

## Required Section Order

1. Hero
2. Professional Summary
3. Engineering Impact
4. Skills and Technology
5. Experience Overview
6. Featured Case Studies
7. Blog Preview
8. Contact

## Section Responsibilities

### Hero

- Shows Evandro’s name
- Shows a concise senior-level headline
- Shows a supporting positioning statement
- Exposes primary calls to action for resume, contact, GitHub, and LinkedIn

### Professional Summary

- Provides a recruiter-friendly summary in English
- Emphasizes seniority, full stack breadth, modernization work, and practical engineering impact

### Engineering Impact

- Displays source-backed achievement cards or list items
- Each item must show context, contribution, impact, and technologies

### Skills and Technology

- Displays grouped, scannable skill categories
- Visually emphasizes the most relevant core skills

### Experience Overview

- Displays concise role summaries rather than a full resume dump
- Keeps business or engineering impact visible for each item

### Featured Case Studies

- Displays preview cards only
- Every preview must be clearly marked as upcoming

### Blog Preview

- Displays future article topics only
- Must not imply live external integration yet

### Contact

- Displays professional contact routes
- Reinforces availability for relevant senior opportunities

## Navigation Expectations

- In-page navigation must point to each required section
- Anchor targets must be stable and human-readable
- Navigation labels must remain concise

## Accessibility Expectations

- Each section must be identifiable through semantic landmarks or headings
- Interactive elements must have descriptive text
- Visual hierarchy must remain understandable without animation
